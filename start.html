New Here? Start Here

Welcome to the El Shaddai family! Whether you just found us on TikTok, watched a drama on YouTube, or someone shared a link with you — this page walks you through your first steps.

Your First Five Steps

Begin Your Journey With Us

1

Watch one drama today

Start with PAMILERIN — our most-loved drama about the price that was paid for you — or MORENIKEJI, a tale of mercy. Both are free on the Watch page. Tap 🤍 to save videos for later.

2

Settle the big question

Many of our dramas end with one question: what will you do with Jesus? If you've never received Him — or you want to return to Him — pray the salvation prayer on our Prayer page, then tap "I Prayed the Prayer" so we can rejoice with you and help you grow.

3

Feed on the Word daily

Visit the Daily Devotional — seven short readings drawn from the messages behind our dramas, plus a Verse of the Day and the weekly prayer focus. Bookmark it; it works even offline.

4

Join the family channels

Subscribe on YouTube and join the WhatsApp Channel — premieres, vigil alerts and prayer updates come there first. Install this site as an app too (look for the 📲 button).

5

Find your place to serve

Got a gift — acting, writing, camera, editing, music, or intercession? Visit Join the Team and apply. Beginners are trained; faithfulness matters more than experience.
