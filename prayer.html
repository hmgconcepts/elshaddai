Give Your Life to Christ

The Most Important Decision

Many of our dramas end with one question: what will you do with Jesus? If today is your day, pray this from your heart:

"Lord Jesus, I come to You as I am. I confess that I am a sinner and I cannot save myself. I believe You died for me and rose again. The price I couldn't pay, You paid for me. Forgive my sins, wash me with Your blood and write my name in the Book of Life. I receive You today as my Lord and Saviour. Thank You for saving me. Amen."

Did you pray this prayer? Please tell us — we want to rejoice with you and help you grow.
