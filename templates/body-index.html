Welcome

Every Drama Is a Sermon. Every Scene Preaches Christ.

El Shaddai Christian Drama Ministry uses gospel drama, short films and inspiring stories to bring the light of God's Word to every home, every phone and every heart — across Nigeria and to the ends of the earth.

🎬

Dramavangelism

Powerful gospel dramas and short movies — like PAMILERIN, MORENIKEJI and TOKUNBO — that confront sin, reveal grace and point souls to Jesus.
