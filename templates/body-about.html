Our Story

From a three-man vigil to a worldwide drama ministry — this is what El Shaddai (God Almighty) can do.

How It Began

The Journey of Faith

2017 — The Vision

God Gives the Vision and the Name

In 2017, God gave the founder a clear vision for a drama ministry and Himself supplied the name: ELSHADDAI — "God Almighty, the All-Sufficient One" (Genesis 17:1). The seed was planted in prayer.

2018 — The Birth

A Three-Day Vigil with Three People

One year after the vision, the ministry began with a three-day vigil on May 7–9, attended by just three people. Like the mustard seed of Matthew 13:31–32, what looked small in man's eyes was mighty in God's hands.

Growth

Drama Becomes the Pulpit

The ministry embraced dramavangelism — preaching the gospel through stage drama and short films that ordinary people connect with: debt, temptation, mercy, redemption and the cross.

Digital Era

ELSHADDAI TV Goes Online

With YouTube (ELSHADDAI TV), Facebook, TikTok, Instagram and a WhatsApp channel, productions like PAMILERIN, MORENIKEJI, TOKUNBO and GBESE now reach viewers far beyond any single hall or city.

Today & Tomorrow

Thy Word Is Light — To the Nations

We keep producing, praying and proclaiming until the light of God's Word shines into every dark place. New projects like THE MIRROR — Behind the Reflection are on the way.

What We Believe

Statement of Faith

📖 The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God — our lamp and our light (Psalm 119:105; 2 Timothy 3:16).

✝️ Salvation in Christ

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in the finished work of Jesus Christ — the price we couldn't pay was paid by Another (Ephesians 2:8–9).

🕊️ The Holy Spirit

We believe in the person and power of the Holy Spirit, who empowers every believer for life and ministry (Acts 1:8).

🌍 The Great Commission

We believe every believer is sent — and that drama, film and media are God-given tools for fulfilling Mark 16:15 in this generation.
