FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I invite the drama team to my church or event?
Use the contact form above (choose "Inviting the drama team"), or WhatsApp the founder directly on 0703 508 3094 with your event details — date, venue, audience and theme. We will respond and pray with you about it.
How do I join the ministry as an actor or crew member?
Send a message via the contact form or WhatsApp telling us your gift — acting, writing, camera, editing, music, prayer or logistics. Every worker is first a believer; we disciple as we deploy.
Are your movies free to watch?
Yes! All productions are released free on YouTube (ELSHADDAI TV), Facebook, TikTok and Instagram. Your subscription, likes and shares are the "payment" that pushes the gospel further.
How was the ministry founded?
God gave the vision and the name ELSHADDAI in 2017. A year later, in 2018, the ministry began with a three-day vigil on May 7–9, attended by just three people. Read the full story on our About page.
How can I support the ministry financially?
Visit the Partner & Give page. For safety, account details are confirmed personally via WhatsApp/call on 0703 508 3094 — never pay into any unverified account.
Can I get prayer or counselling privately?
Absolutely. Use the Prayer page and tick "keep my request confidential", or contact the founder directly. Your matter is handled with love and discretion.
